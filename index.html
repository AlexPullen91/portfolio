About Me

I am a creative person; whether it was building TIE fighters out of Lego when I was little, writing songs about my favourite flavour of nuts on guitar, or providing lighting for theatres and live events in my previous career, I've always needed a creative outlet.

That is why I'm drawn to coding - the ability to build almost anything you can think of just so long as you can imagine it.

By undertaking a formal education in full stack web development, I am now equipped with the skills to bring my ideas to life.

Going forwards, I'd like to further develop my understanding of JavaScript and build more sophisticated, aesthetically pleasing websites, apps and games.
